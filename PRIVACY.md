# Privacy Policy for LaTomate

**Last Updated:** November 1, 2025

## Data Collection

LaTomate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Local Storage

All timer data, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your computer and is not transmitted to any server.

## Permissions

LaTomate requires the following permissions:
- **Storage**: To save your timer state and preferences locally
- **Notifications**: To notify you when work sessions or breaks are complete
- **Alarms**: To manage timer functionality in the background

## Third-Party Services

LaTomate does not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or external APIs.

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally and can be cleared at any time by:
1. Uninstalling the extension
2. Clearing browser data through Chrome settings
